About SquadSync

We are a webpage dedicated to simplify and streamline the intramural sports world. We want to focus on giving people an all-in-one place for their teams, leagues, and tournaments with simple tools to better their experience.

Other platforms can be complicated or have too many hoops to jump through just for you to fit on a roster or manage a team. We want to be different. We want to be better.

Our Goal: We want to create a place for all sports players, athletes or not, to have a stress free place to play the sports they love. Also, making tools that anyone can learn and understand with the least stress is our top priority.
